Collections are made up of blocks and are like mini-dialogues that you put together to make dialogues. Each has one (and only one) "event": an action that starts it, like dials in or hitting a schedule.

Bits that imply they need to be "filled in" are show with a dotted outline.

Filters are used to slice up groups of contacts and their answer. Contacts have labels applied to them, and their answers to questions are saved using "Save Answer As" blocks.

There are dismissable help bits all over the place. In testing, we found that they proved useful when the user read them (which was often when coming back to a bit of the interface).
